## Brand and asset intelligence

**Palette:**
- The public logo uses a warm orange upper mark, a vivid blue lower mark, charcoal/black wordmark, and white space.
- Campaign CSS uses close visual approximations (`#ff653f`, `#3f73e6`, `#16171d`) because no public brand-guideline document establishing canonical hex values was found. These are campaign colors, not asserted official specifications.

**Typography character:**
- Clean modern sans serif; high legibility; sentence-case product language; restrained weight contrast.

**Photography / illustration character:**
- Utility field operations, dispatch, crews, routes, and business users; practical rather than futuristic.
- The campaign uses original diagrams and CSS-rendered operational UI instead of reusing customer or product screenshots.

**Geometry and interface motifs:**
- Route lines, work-order cards, pins, connections, rounded controls, clean white surfaces, and clear status hierarchy.

**Overall vibe:**
- Modern, operational, practical, trustworthy, utility-focused, and outcome-oriented.
